Hip Hop 1972 CrazySexyCool by TLC Hip Hop music is a rhythmically-centered music that usually serves as a layer over which artists rap, a rhythmic, rhyming chanting. It was developed by African Americans in the United states during the 1970s when block parties grew in popularity, especially in New York City. It is synonymous with rap music and incorporates sampling parts of songs, bass lines, and the use of drum machines.
