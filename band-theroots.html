The Roots

The Roots is an American Hip Hop group known fro their live musical performances and their jazzy approach to hip hop. The band originated in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania by Tariq “Black Thought” Trotter and Ahmir “Questlove” Thompson while both were attending Philadelphia High School for the Creative and Performing Arts. Since their formation, the band has released 10 studio albums, 2 EPs, and 2 collaboration albums( with artist John Legend and Betty Wright). The band has undergone several member changes as the total member of the band equates to 16 current and previous members. The band is currently serves as the house band for the Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon.
